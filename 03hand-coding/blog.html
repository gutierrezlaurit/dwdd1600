Buddha Bowl

Restaurant: Harvest, Park City, Utah

The Sundance Film Festival just ended, and the town is starting to settle down. On my recent visit to Park City, I stopped by Harvest, a cozy café known for three things: Food, Coffee, and People.

In the morning, it’s a popular spot for breakfast, but if you're looking for a light yet satisfying meal, I highly recommend the Buddha Bowl. It’s a refreshing and filling salad, bursting with an explosion of flavors that blend perfectly together.
