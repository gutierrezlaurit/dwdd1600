Alfajores de Maizena

  • 200 gr of Salted Butter
  • 200 gr of flour
  • 300 gr of Cornstarch
  • 1/2 tsp of Baking Soda
  • 2 tsp of Baking Powder
  • 150 gr of Powder Sugar
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1 tbsp of cognac
  • 1 tsp of Vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp of lemon juice
  • Dulce de leche (for filling)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, beat the butter with the sugar until smooth.
  2. Add the yolks one at a time, mixing everything well.
  3. Add dry ingredients, mix gently without kneading.
  4. Cut cookies and bake at moderate heat for 15 minutes.
  5. Fill with dulce de leche and roll in grated coconut.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is an alfajor?
A South American dessert that features two melt-in-your-mouth cookies with dulce de leche sandwiched between them.
What is dulce de leche?
Dulce de leche is a thick, rich caramel sauce made from sweetened condensed milk.
